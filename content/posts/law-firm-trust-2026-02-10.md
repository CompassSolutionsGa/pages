If you rely on inbound leads, your law firm contact form is one of your highest-impact pages.

## Recommended form fields for Law Firm
- Full name
- Phone number
- Email (optional)
- Case type (injury, criminal, family, immigration, civil)
- City / ZIP code
- Best time to contact
- Preferred contact method (call/text/email)
- Brief case summary (optional)

## Example questions to include
- What type of legal help do you need?
- What happened (high-level summary)?
- When did this occur?
- Is there an upcoming court date or deadline?
- Whatâ€™s the best way and time to reach you?

## A/B tests worth running
- Test CTA: Get a free case review vs Talk to an attorney
- Test allowing text-first contact vs call-only
- Test case-type buttons vs dropdown
- Test short form + follow-up intake vs long form
- Test adding urgency prompt ("Any deadlines?") vs none
