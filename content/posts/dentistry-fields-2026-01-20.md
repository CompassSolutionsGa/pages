If you rely on inbound leads, your dentistry contact form is one of your highest-impact pages.

## Recommended form fields for Dentistry
- Full name
- Phone number
- Email (optional)
- Appointment type (cleaning, consultation, emergency, cosmetic)
- New patient? (yes/no)
- Insurance provider (optional)
- Preferred days/times
- Location preference (if multiple offices)

## A/B tests worth running
- Test CTA: Book an appointment vs Request availability
- Test asking insurance info (optional) vs not asking at all
- Test "New patient special" mention vs no mention
- Test shorter form vs appointment-type-first flow
- Test offering call-back within 15 minutes vs generic follow-up
